New launch of security against bots or malicious websites: HENRY GLOBAL CEO - HENRY KAMSI OKWUABUDIKE, spent a lot of effort developing a Security GateKeepper to not just protect their apps or website, but to provide a Security Gatekeeper for websites/web apps, Android Apps, and iOS (SWIFT) to be able to integrate free of charge.

Privacy, Ethics, and User Trust

In an era where personal data is often treated as a commodity, HGT draws a firm line in the sand. We are deeply committed to respecting user privacy. Our policy is simple: we do not hide code, and we do not perform "background" actions that the user hasn't explicitly authorized. We operate on a model of total transparency. Before any action is performed, our applications communicate clearly with the user, requesting permission and explaining exactly why that permission is necessary.

We believe that trust is the most valuable currency in the digital age. Many companies choose to harvest data behind the scenes, using complex algorithms to track behavior without consent. At HGT, we find this practice unacceptable. We never do anything behind the scene. Every process that runs on your device under our brand is there because you allowed it to be there. This ethical stance is not just a policy; it is the core of our brand identity. We respect your autonomy, your security, and your right to know how your information is being handled.

This commitment extends to our data ethics. We believe that a user’s data belongs to the user alone. Our servers are built with security-first architecture to protect this sanctity. When you grant us permission to access a feature or service, it is a contract of trust that we take very seriously. We invite our users to hold us accountable, to audit our processes, and to engage with us on the topic of digital rights. By putting the power back into the hands of the user, we are fostering a digital environment where people feel safe, secure, and valued. Your privacy is not an afterthought at Henry Global Tech Industry; it is the foundation upon which all our software is built.

No telemetry without consent. Unlike big tech firms, HGT apps will never send usage data unless you explicitly opt-in for improvement programs. We are GDPR compliant by design and we extend those protections to every user, regardless of region.
